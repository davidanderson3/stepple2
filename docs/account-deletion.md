# Stepple Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Stepple account and remove all associated data (including step history, profile information, and friend connections), contact our support team using one of the options below:

- Email: [privacy@stepple.com](mailto:privacy@stepple.com)
- In-app: open **Settings → Help → Contact Support** and include the subject "Delete my account"

Please provide the Stepple user ID shown on the Settings screen, or the email address you used to sign in. We will confirm the request and delete your data within 7 days.

If you reinstall Stepple after your account has been deleted, you will start with a new account.

For any questions, reach us at [privacy@stepple.com](mailto:privacy@stepple.com).
